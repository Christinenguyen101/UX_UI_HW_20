My Projects

U.S. Department of the Interior

User Reascarch

As a researcher I want to know what does user think about the U.S. department of interior. I also did some interviews and usability test and people are frustrated that there are so many dead end links, lots of information (overwhelming), and also some users are interested in joining but find it hard to find the contact info beside social media.

Define and Synthesis

I made a affinity diagram gather all the information and insight that I have received. it help me define what the problems are and I'm trying to re design this website to make it look more lively , don't have over whelming information and have a easier signing up page.

Ideation

I have some ideas about how I wanted to redesign my website with the help of making a mood board and creating a site map that can help me organize the categories and make it easier for user to navigate through the website. then I started to do some digital wireframing.

Prototype

When I was doing the the wireframes, I made the color scheme that has blue and green. add more pictures, and i made the categories easier to understand and navigate. I went onto Invision to make it clickable. I asked people to go through the porotype to give me some feedback.

User Testing

During the user testing, I got really good feedback on how can I improve the website better also users likes and dislike. I only did the user testing to 5 people and all of them like the color scheme. They also completed the given task which was what is the department of interior is about and finding the contact page without a hassle.

Future Opportunities

I want to change more of the minor details that i didn't do in the first place on my redesign
